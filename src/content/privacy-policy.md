# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: January 11, 2026**

## Introduction

Welcome to FindrHub. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share your information when you use our services.

## Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us, such as when you create an account, post a report, or contact us for support. This may include:

- Name and contact information
- Profile information
- Content you post (reports, comments, images)
- Geolocation data (with your permission)

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

- Provide and maintain our services
- Facilitate the return of lost items
- Communicate with you about your account or reports
- Improve our platform and user experience

## Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with other users as part of the normal operation of the service (e.g., displaying your report to others) or with service providers who help us operate our platform.

## Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information. You can manage most of these settings directly within your account profile.

## Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at [privacy@findrhub.com](/contact).
